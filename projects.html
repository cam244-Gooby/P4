Seamless Gutter Installation

Project Details

This residential project in Buildtown involved the complete replacement of an outdated gutter system with new seamless gutters and downspouts. The homeowners were experiencing water damage to their foundation due to improper drainage.

Our team installed 6-inch seamless gutters with oversized downspouts to handle heavy rainfall. We also added gutter guards to prevent clogging from leaves and debris. The new system effectively channels water away from the home's foundation, preventing further damage.

Project Highlights

  • 6-inch seamless aluminum gutters
  • Oversized downspouts for improved water flow
  • Gutter guards to prevent clogging
  • Proper slope for efficient drainage
  • Matching color to complement the home's exterior

The homeowners were extremely satisfied with the results and have reported no further issues with water drainage around their home.

Roof Replacement

Project Details

This residential project in Roofington involved a complete roof replacement for a home with an aging and damaged roof. The homeowners were experiencing leaks and wanted to improve their home's appearance and protection.

Our team removed the old shingles and inspected the roof deck for damage. We replaced damaged sections of the deck and installed new underlayment, ice and water shield, and premium architectural shingles. We also improved the ventilation system to extend the life of the new roof.

Project Highlights

  • Premium architectural shingles with a 30-year warranty
  • Complete tear-off of old roofing materials
  • Deck repair where needed
  • Improved ventilation system
  • New flashing around chimneys and vents

The homeowners were thrilled with their new roof, which has enhanced their home's curb appeal and provided reliable protection against the elements.
